"Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire" is a 2023 Indian action thriller film directed by Prashanth Neel, starring Prabhas, Prithviraj Sukumaran, Shruti Haasan, and Easwari Rao. The story follows Deva and Vardha, childhood friends who become enemies in the war-torn land of Khansaar, a fictional city-state. Deva, known as Cutout, lives a quiet life, while Vardha, the son of Raja Mannar, is destined to become the ruler, a decision that triggers a coup. The film explores themes of power, brotherhood, and the consequences of violence in a dystopian setting

Plot: In Khansaar, Raja Mannar's decision to make his son, Vardha, his successor sparks a dangerous coup, leading to a chaotic environment with foreign armies hired to cause chaos. Radha, Raja Mannar's daughter and the current ruler, announces a ceasefire, but the situation remains volatile.
Characters:
Deva (Prabhas): A gentle, unassuming mechanic who is secretly a powerful warrior, willing to fight for his friend Vardha. Vardha (Prithviraj Sukumaran): A prince whose life is threatened, and who turns to his childhood friend, Deva, for help. Radha (Sriya Reddy): The ruler of Khansaar, who attempts to maintain peace amidst the chaos.
The film's initial storyline was pitched from Neel's debut film Ugramm (2014) and is the maiden part of a two-part film.[3] It was officially announced in December 2020 under the title Salaar, however, in July 2023, its first instalment was titled as Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire. Principal photography commenced in January 2021, and occurred sporadically in several legs over nearly three years, before wrapping in late 2023. Filming locations included Telangana, Italy and Budapest. Production difficulties, ranging from the pandemic, reshoots and VFX delays, postponed Salaar's release date several times. The music is composed by Ravi Basrur, cinematography handled by Bhuvan Gowda and editing by Ujwal Kulkarni.
